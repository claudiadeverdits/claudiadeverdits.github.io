My Final Project

This is my final project, Decorate A Christmas Tree that was designed in JavaScript and ProcessingJS.
In this game, you're simply decorating a christmas tree with many customization options. The game starts off on a home screen where you can choose the color of your tree from 6 choices: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, or purple. Once you make your choice, you are taken to a new screen with your tree. On this screen there are multiple buttons for more customization options. There are 3 buttons to choose a topper from the tree, either a star, or the choice of 2 bitmojis. You are also able to choose the ornaments you want to place on the tree. By clicking on the ornament buttons, you will get a random color of that type of ornament every time. In the event you want to restart on the same tree, there is a clear button for you to use, and if you want to choose a new color tree, simply press the "go back" button to be taken to the home screen.
