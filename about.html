About Me

My name is Claudia Deverdits and I am a first year student at the University of Rhode Island with a major in Computer Science and a minor in both Cyber Security and General Business. During the summer of 2021 I was accepted into a coding camp, Kode with Klossy, where I learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript over the course of 2 weeks and created a fully functioning professional website with a team.
